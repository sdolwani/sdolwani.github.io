How does In-Network Insurance work?

Life Couseling Clinic has paternered with the leading insurance companies to offer both In-Network insurance along with optional out-of-network. For Out-of-Network options, a monthly clinical superbill can be submitted to your insurance company.

How does Out-of-Network Insurance work?

You pay up front for the session with the acceptable payments. Then, you will receive a clinical superbill, which includes everything an insurance company would need to assess possible reimbursement (service codes, diagnostic impression, license numbers, EIN and NPI information). Then you must check with your individual benefits to see what Out-of-Network options are available for you. If available, you can then submit it to your insurance company.

How do I know if I'm Eligible for Insurance?

Reaching out to your individual, business, and family heatlh insurance marketplace plans should help clarify if you are eligible for either type of insurance. For more information on insurance benefits, visit the APA to learn more about mental health coverage and insurance.

Focuses

Trauma Focus

Every single individual on our team is able to provide trauma focused approaches. Reprocessing and learning to live without ignoring trauma can enable individuals to thrive throughout life.

Individual Focus

Again, each counselor at our clinic has been trained in indivdal care. If you are interested in a specific counselor, book now to find out what date and time works best for both of you.

Couple's Focus

Some counselors have specialized training in relationship, marriage, and pre-marital counseling. These sessions involve deepening understanding within one's relationship. Mixing in family and couples counseling services as needed is also an option.

Family Focus

For children, estranged relatives, or a struggling family, a family focus approach where all participants are involved is also an option. Working on the entire family can help minimize individual struggles within the family system.
