Reprocess your past

Reflection on the past has a powerful effect to help uncover what has been learned through development. Revisiting past trauma and understanding how it causes certain decisions to be made can help give a more open mind.

Work through the present

Feeling stuck or overwhelmed is perfectly normal. Regardless of your unique story, all clients have problems in the present that seem daunting, and we are here to help. Life Counseling Clinic aims to retrain the nervous system through multiple methods. Healthy coping mechanisms, proper emotion identification, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, and more help our clients live in the moment instead of in their mind.

Control your Future

The future can be uncertain, and we're here to help. Counselors can help clients better understand purpose, motivation, and options available to each individual's concerns. Counselors at Life Counseling Clinic work with a licensed social work (LCSW) counselor who can weigh in, if explicit permission is given, to provide unique resources and connections in your area.

Life Cousneling Clinic Specializations

A variety of empeircally proven methods are available. While you might not have the exact disorder, consider the 5 most common disorder types below to find the counselor that can fit best for you.

Focused Disorders Symptoms Empirical Treatment
Anxiety Disorders (GAD, SAD, OCD, PTSD, Social Anxiety Disorder, Panic Disorder, Phobias) Frequent distress/fear/apprehension; distress during non-stressful events (school, work, etc.) CBT, psychotherapy, ERP, exposure therapy
Mood Disorders (Major Depressive Disorder, Bipolar Disorder, Dysthymia, Substance-induced Mood Disorder) Ongoing sad/empty feeling; feelings of hopelessness, guilt; lack of self-esteem; decreased energy MDD, IPT, CBT, BT, EFT, antidepressants
Psychotic Disorders (Schizophrenia, Schizoaffective Disorder, Delusional Disorder) Trouble discerning reality from perceived reality CBT, psychotherapy, anti-psychotics
Eating Disorders (Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia Nervosa, Binge Eating Disorder) Mental development of unhealthy eating habits CBT, psychotherapy, nutrition education, medical monitoring, vitamins
Dementia (Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, Frontotemporal dementia, Huntington’s, Wernicke-Korsakoff) Declination in cognitive abilities; issues with memory and thinking skills CBT, occupational therapy, physical therapy, donepezil, rivastigmine, galantamine,
